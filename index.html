Instructions

After being on the run from monsters for days, you've run into a dead end. With the your remaining energy, destroy as many skeletons as you can! Will you be overwhlemed, or will you survive?


After every 10 skeletons you destroy, the new ones will get stronger. Once you run out of health, its game over!
